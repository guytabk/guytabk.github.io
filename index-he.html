Why I Love the Simple Web
July 28, 2025

There's something beautiful about the simplicity of early web design. No JavaScript frameworks, no complex build processes - just HTML, CSS, and pure content. Pages load instantly, work on any device, and will probably still work in 20 years.

Sometimes the old ways are the best ways. This blog is my tribute to that philosophy.
